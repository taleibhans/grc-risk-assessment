# LuxeOnGo — IT Risk Assessment & Compliance Review

**Author:** Talei  
**Version:** 1.0 — 2025-11-21

## About
This repo contains a practical GRC portfolio project for **LuxeOnGo** — a beauty business operating an ecommerce store, on-the-go services, and automated vending machines. The deliverables demonstrate risk assessment, corporate policy writing, and a mapped compliance checklist (ISO 27001 & NIST CSF).

## Contents
- `Risk_Assessment/01_LuxeOnGo_Risk_Assessment.pdf` — detailed 5-page risk assessment
- `Policies/02_LuxeOnGo_AUP.pdf` — 4-page Acceptable Use Policy
- `Compliance/03_LuxeOnGo_ISO27001_Checklist.xlsx` — 4-page compliance checklist with explanations
- `docs/images/` — supporting diagrams and visuals (example: `A_compliance_checklist_and_control_explanations_do.png`)
- `templates/` — risk register & vendor questionnaire templates
- `docs/INTERVIEW-TALKING-POINTS.md` — concise script to explain the project in interviews

## Key highlights
- Mapped controls to ISO 27001 Annex A & NIST CSF
- Prioritized remediation plan (MFA, patching, logging, backups)
- Practical policy (AUP) tailored to retail + POS + vending environment
